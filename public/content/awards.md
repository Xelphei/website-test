# Awards & Recognition

We believe in celebrating excellence and recognizing the outstanding contributions of professionals in our field.

## Annual Awards

### Professional of the Year
Awarded to an individual who has demonstrated exceptional leadership, innovation, and impact in the profession over the past year.

### Rising Star Award
Recognizes an early-career professional (5 years or fewer in the field) who shows exceptional promise and has already made significant contributions.

### Community Impact Award
Honors an individual or team whose work has made a meaningful positive impact on the broader community.

### Lifetime Achievement Award
Celebrates a distinguished professional whose career-long contributions have shaped and advanced the field.
